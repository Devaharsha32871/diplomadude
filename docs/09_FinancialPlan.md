# Financial Plan

## Revenue Model
1. **Freemium Access:** Basic AI usage for free, premium plan for unlimited access.  
2. **Affiliate Content:** Commission from recommended courses or videos.  
3. **Institutional Licensing:** Subscription plans for colleges and organizations.

## Resource Distribution
| Area | Allocation |
|-------|-------------|
| AI Development | 40% |
| Infrastructure | 25% |
| Design & UI | 20% |
| Marketing | 15% |

## Long-Term Goals
- Expand to other educational boards.  
- Introduce institution-specific AI dashboards.  
- Offer certification-based learning modules.
